About Love All Serve All Inc

LOVE ALL SERVE ALL INC is a global humanitarian foundation providing Nutrition, Education, and Healthcare totally free of cost to underserved communities! Love All, Serve All is the core value around which Sadguru Sri Madhusudan Sai, a globally recognized humanitarian and spiritual teacher has designed the Global Humanitarian Mission.

The Global Humanitarian Mission is a group of public service institutions run by individuals who are (or were) corporate professionals, teachers, doctors, para-medicals, scientists, business entrepreneurs, and other skilled professionals from around the world. We are headquartered in California, USA.
